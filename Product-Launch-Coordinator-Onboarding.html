Key Rules & Shoot Capacity

Shoot Day Capacity

  • Studio (E-comm): ~25–30 products per day
  • Shooting starts ~9–9:30am, wraps ~5pm
  • ~15 min per product (on-model)
  • Lifestyle/outdoors: budget until 11am
  • Account for 1 hr lunch
  • First off-figure shot: ~30 min setup
  • Detail shots (pockets, zippers): varies
  • Hero shots with props: 30–45 min each

Photography Rules

  • Any item launching without existing photos needs a shoot
  • All products need: Studio + Off-Figure
  • Hero products need: add Hub, consider Lifestyle
  • Lifestyle shot done once per product — not for every new colour
  • Don't over-rely on recolours: site starts looking repetitive
  • Always notify team (Slack) when launch goes live
  • When in doubt on timing: ask Noa directly

Decision Prompts

  • Do we already have a studio shot of this item?
  • Is this a new colour or a new product?
  • Will retouching still look premium and accurate?
  • Is the website becoming oversaturated with near-identical imagery?
  • Is sample arriving in time for the planned shoot?
  • Does the hero product need campaign-level lifestyle?

Photography Types Defined

  • Studio (E-comm) — on-model product shot
  • Off-Figure — pinups, flat lays, fabric close-ups, detail shots (buttons/zippers/pockets)
  • Hub — hybrid: fabricated backgrounds + props, lifestyle-esque in studio
  • Lifestyle — on-location, conceptual, campaign-level
